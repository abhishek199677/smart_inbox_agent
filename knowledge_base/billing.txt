MEDICAL BILLING INFORMATION

Common Billing Codes (CPT):
- 99213: Office visit, established patient, moderate complexity
- 99214: Office visit, established patient, high complexity
- 99396: Periodic preventive medicine, 40-64 years
- 93000: Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG)
- 81001: Urinalysis with microscopic exam

BILLING QUESTIONS

If you have questions about a bill:
1. Verify the service date matches what you received
2. Check that your insurance has been applied
3. Look for the "patient responsibility" amount
4. Contact the billing department if anything seems incorrect

PAYMENT OPTIONS

We offer several payment options:
- Pay in full online via the member portal
- Set up a monthly payment plan (no interest for 12 months)
- Apply for financial assistance if you meet income guidelines

PAYMENT PLAN TERMS

Standard payment plans:
- 3 months: No interest, no fees
- 6 months: 2% interest, no fees
- 12 months: 4% interest, $5 monthly fee

To set up a payment plan, log into the member portal or call billing at 1-800-555-BILL.

REFUND POLICY

Overpayments are refunded within 14 business days.
Refunds are processed back to the original payment method when possible.
For check refunds, allow 21 business days for delivery.

INSURANCE COORDINATION OF BENEFITS

If you have multiple insurance policies:
- Primary insurance pays first
- Secondary insurance may cover remaining costs
- You must provide all insurance information at time of service
- Medicare is typically primary for patients 65+
