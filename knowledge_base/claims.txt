CLAIMS PROCESSING GUIDE

To file a new health insurance claim, you need:
1. A completed claim form (available on the member portal)
2. Itemized bill from your healthcare provider
3. Proof of payment (if already paid)
4. Your insurance policy number and group ID

Claims are typically processed within 15-30 business days.
You can check claim status online at the member portal or by calling 1-800-555-CLAIM.

CLAIM DENIALS

Common reasons for claim denial:
- Service not covered under your plan
- Missing prior authorization
- Out-of-network provider
- Coding errors on the claim
- Deductible not yet met

If your claim is denied:
1. Review the denial reason on your Explanation of Benefits (EOB)
2. Gather supporting documentation
3. File an appeal within 180 days of the denial date
4. Appeals are reviewed within 30 days for active treatment, 60 days otherwise

APPEAL PROCESS

Level 1: Internal appeal with the insurance company
Level 2: External independent review if Level 1 is denied

You have the right to request an expedited appeal if waiting could seriously jeopardize your health.

PRIOR AUTHORIZATION

Prior authorization is required for:
- MRI and CT scans
- Surgical procedures
- Specialty medications
- Inpatient hospital stays

Prior authorization requests are typically reviewed within 72 hours for urgent cases, 15 days for non-urgent.
