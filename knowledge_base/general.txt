MEMBER PORTAL GUIDE

The member portal allows you to:
- View claims and EOB documents
- Check deductible and out-of-pocket progress
- Find in-network providers
- Request ID cards
- Update personal information
- Set up auto-pay for premiums

To register: Go to portal.healthinsurance.com and click "Register".
You will need your policy number, date of birth, and ZIP code.

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

To update your address, phone, or email:
1. Log into the member portal
2. Go to Profile Settings
3. Make changes and save
4. Changes take effect within 24 hours

To add a dependent (new baby, marriage):
- Report within 60 days of the qualifying event
- Provide documentation (birth certificate, marriage license)
- Coverage is effective from the date of the event

COVERAGE QUESTIONS

Coverage year runs January 1 to December 31.
Annual deductibles reset each calendar year.
Preventive care (annual physical, vaccines) is covered at 100%.
Telehealth visits are covered at the same rate as in-office visits.

TECHNICAL SUPPORT

Common technical issues and solutions:
- Login problems: Clear browser cache or reset password
- Portal not loading: Try Chrome or Firefox (latest versions)
- Mobile app issues: Update to the latest version
- EOB not displaying: Allow 24-48 hours after claim processing

For technical support, call 1-800-555-TECH or use the live chat on the portal.

OPEN ENROLLMENT

Open enrollment runs November 1 - January 15 each year.
During open enrollment you can:
- Change your plan
- Add or remove dependents
- Update coverage levels
- Change your primary care provider

Special enrollment periods (60 days) are available for:
- Marriage, divorce, or death
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Loss of other coverage
- Moving to a new coverage area
